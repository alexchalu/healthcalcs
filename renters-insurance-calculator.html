About Renters Insurance

Renters insurance protects your personal belongings and provides liability coverage. Most landlords require it, and it's surprisingly affordable — often $15-30/month.

What It Covers:

  • Personal Property: Your belongings (furniture, electronics, clothes)
  • Liability: Legal costs if someone is injured in your rental
  • Additional Living Expenses: Hotel costs if your rental is uninhabitable
  • Medical Payments: Guest injuries (small amounts)

Factors Affecting Cost:

  • Value of personal property
  • Liability coverage amount ($100k-$500k typical)
  • Deductible (higher deductible = lower premium)
  • Location (crime rate, natural disaster risk)
  • Claims history
  • Security features (alarm systems, deadbolts)

Tip: Bundle with auto insurance for 10-25% discounts!
