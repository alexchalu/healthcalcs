Understanding Auto Insurance Coverage

Every state requires minimum liability coverage, shown as three numbers (e.g., 25/50/25):

  • First number: Bodily injury per person (in thousands)
  • Second number: Bodily injury per accident (in thousands)
  • Third number: Property damage per accident (in thousands)

Most financial experts recommend carrying at least 100/300/100 to protect your assets.
