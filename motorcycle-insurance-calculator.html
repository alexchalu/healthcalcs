About Motorcycle Insurance

Motorcycle insurance costs vary widely based on bike type, rider age, experience, and location. Sport bikes cost 2-3x more to insure than cruisers.

Coverage Types:

  • Liability: Damage/injury you cause to others (required in most states)
  • Collision: Damage to your bike from accidents
  • Comprehensive: Theft, vandalism, weather damage
  • Uninsured Motorist: Protection if hit by uninsured driver
  • Medical Payments: Your medical bills after accident

Ways to Save:

  • Take a motorcycle safety course (5-15% discount)
  • Bundle with auto/home insurance
  • Store bike in a garage
  • Install anti-theft devices
  • Choose higher deductibles
  • Limit annual mileage

Sport Bike Riders: Expect to pay 2-4x more than cruiser riders due to higher accident rates.
